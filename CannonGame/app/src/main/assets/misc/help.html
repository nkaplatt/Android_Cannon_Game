How to play:

Aim

The aim of the game is to shoot the target pieces whilst avoiding the blockades. Starting the game puts 10 seconds on the clock, for every target hit 3 seconds is added to the clock, for every blockade hit 2 seconds is deducted.

Instructions

Touch the screen to aim the cannon in that direction, the cannon will fire a cannon ball in a straight line in the direction of your touch.

Scores

For each target hit you are rewarded 10 X's the difficulty level, hitting a blockade decreases your scored by 15 X difficulty. E.g. On level two a target will reward 20 points and a blockade will deduct 30.
